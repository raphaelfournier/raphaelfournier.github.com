About (fr)

Hello, I'm Raphaël, a post-doctoral student from Paris, France. I currently work at the [L2TI][] laboratory, with [Prof. Emmanuel Viennet][emmanuel], on the AMMICO project (which aims at improving museums exhibition guiding devices).

I did my Ph. D at the [LIP6][] laboratory with the [ComplexNetworks][www-cn] team, under the supervision of [Matthieu Latapy][matthieu]. You may download a [PDF version of the dissertation][these] (document in French). My research interests are related to P2P networks and Internet measurement, and data mining. I also studied paedophile activity in P2P networks. For more information, you may want to have a look at my resume.
