Hello, I'm Raphaël, a post-doctoral student from Paris, France. I currently work at the L2TI laboratory, with Prof. Emmanuel Viennet, on the AMMICO project (which aims at improving museums exhibition guiding devices).

I did my Ph. D at the LIP6 laboratory with the ComplexNetworks team, under the supervision of Matthieu Latapy. You may download a PDF version of the dissertation (document in French). My research interests are related to P2P networks and Internet measurement, and data mining. I also studied paedophile activity in P2P networks. For more information, you may want to have a look at my resume.
